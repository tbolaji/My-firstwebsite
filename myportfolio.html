Information: The Language of Everything

Information has been described as something that reduces uncertainly and every one needs information, because of the importance of information in decision making, the use of information is essential, and every individual is involved in variety of activities that requires decision making, it means every individual need information. Information will certainly resolve any question that may arise and this gives anyone who has access to information a completive advantage, so therefore the usefulness of information cannot be over emphasized. Ekoja (2010) said information is required for individual growth and development and by extension, societal growth and change. He further reiterates that information is needed for personal, social and/or work-related activities of individuals and for the development of societies and states.
